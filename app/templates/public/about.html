About Archives World Map

Archives World Map is a geospatial collaborative platform for cataloging public archive institutions worldwide.

Anyone can contribute by adding institutions to the map. Submissions are reviewed before being published.

After being created and maintained for several years within the CRIDI research group, Archives World Map is now being updated and maintained within the Laboratory of Digital Humanities of UFBA (LABHDUFBA).

Created and maintained by Ricardo Sodré Andrade.
